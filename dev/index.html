Welcome to the family.

Our names are Gary and Heidi Bailey. We have been married for six years and have two wonderful daughters Louella (4) and Evette (2). We have raised our kids to be very respectful of animals. We also have two cats.

We love animals!

Animals have always been a part of our daily life, dogs and cats alike. They all have their own unique personalities and beauty. Getting to know your animal and building a relationship with them is one of the most rewarding parts about doing what we do.

Bath and training included.

Many dog sitting services upcharge for baths and training. We believe both are simply part of the dog sitting experience. Just let us know if your pup would like a bath or if there are specific areas of training your dog needs help with like leash pulling and house training.
